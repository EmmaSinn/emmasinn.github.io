Emma Sinn's Projects from the University of St. Thomas

Last semester, I took the senior capstone where we made a product that acted as a virtural waiter. I worked on the database team and together with out other teams, we created, AutoGarcon. AutoGarcon featured an app where the customers could order from while they sat at a table in a sit-down restruant. They also could order off an Amazon Alexa that would be placed at a table for the customer's convenience. The website was for the restruant staff. It allowed the cooks to access the orders so the food could be prepared and for the management to be able to make changes to a bill and to the items available to a restruant. With my team, we were in charge of setting up and modifying the database. We were in charge of all the data our product needed to store. We also set up the interface between the database and the other three teams so the website, the app, and Alexa's could access the necessary information.
